Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to common questions about the application process.

General Questions

National, Regional and International students are eligible to apply.

  • New and Transfer Applicants: Apply online through the COSTAATT Online Application Portal.
  • Re-Admission Applicants: Download the Re-Admission Application Form, complete it, and email it.
  • Online Applications: Free
  • Re-Admission Applications (Electronic): $10.00 TTD
  • Paper Applications: $25.00 TTD

Document Submission

  • General Documents: Valid ID (TT ID card or passport), birth certificate, and academic certificates.
  • Program-Specific Documents: Character references, police certificates, nursing council permits, or job letter (depending on the programme).

Email Us to request an extension. applications@costaatt.edu.tt

Email documents in JPEG or PDF format to applications@costaatt.edu.tt

GATE and Financial Aid

The Government Assistance for Tuition Expenses (GATE) programme provides financial support/assistance with tuition fees to eligible citizens of Trinidad and Tobago. For eligibility and application details, visit the Ministry of Education website.

Yes, COSTAATT offers flexible payment plans. Contact the Office of the Bursar for details.

Yes, COSTAATT offers scholarships for academic excellence, leadership, and financial need. Contact the Financial Aid Office for more information

Programme Specific Questions

Nursing Applicants must submit:

  • Two written character references
  • A police certificate of character
  • A Nursing Council permit

Contact the Admissions Office to discuss changing your programme. You may need to submit a new application or additional documents.

Yes, COSTAATT accepts transfer credits on a case-by-case basis. Submit your transcripts and course descriptions for evaluation.

After Applying

You’ll receive an email with your admission decision and next steps, including registration instructions.

Contact the Office of the Registrar or visit your campus for assistance.

Yes, you may request a deferral by contacting the Admissions Office. Deferrals are subject to approval.

International Students

Yes, international students are welcome to apply. Additional documents, such as a valid passport and student visa, may be required.

COSTAATT does not provide on-campus housing, but the Admissions Office can assist with finding accommodation options.

Tuition fees for international students vary by programme. For a detailed breakdown, download the Tuition and Fees Guide.
